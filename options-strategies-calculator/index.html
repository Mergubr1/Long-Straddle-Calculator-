Assumptions & Notes

  • All figures are illustrative — replace inputs with your own trade data.
  • Values at expiration use intrinsic value only (no Black-Scholes / time-value pricing before expiration).
  • Covered strategies compare "Stock Shares Owned / Short" against shares controlled by the options and warn if the position isn't fully covered.
  • Return on Capital uses Margin/Collateral if entered; otherwise it uses the debit paid or stock investment as the base.
  • Fees are applied once to the overall position, not per contract.
  • Everything recalculates live as you edit inputs or switch strategy — nothing is hardcoded.
